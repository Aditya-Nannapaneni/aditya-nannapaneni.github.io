## Context

Teams needed to retrieve relevant account context and recommend next actions from heterogeneous enterprise data. Keyword search and static rules were insufficient.

## My contribution

I built semantic retrieval and recommendation components, including embedding and ranking interfaces used in production decision workflows.

## System design

- **Inputs:** Enterprise account and opportunity records and related signals.
- **Outputs:** Retrieved context and ranked recommendations for operators.
- **Major components:** Embedding generation, retrieval index, ranking, and evaluation harnesses.
- **Deployment boundary:** Internal AWS systems.

This case study describes the system at a high level and excludes confidential AWS data, implementation details, and customer information.

## Key technical decisions

1. **Dense retrieval versus rules** — dense retrieval improved recall for nuanced account context.
2. **Evaluation before index expansion** — retrieval quality was measured against task-relevant labels where available.
3. **Shared embedding infrastructure** — avoided per-project embedding pipelines.

## Evaluation

Offline retrieval and ranking evaluation against task-specific relevance criteria, plus production monitoring. Proprietary corpora and exact index topology are omitted.

## Results

Shipped production retrieval and recommendation systems used in decision workflows. Exact proprietary performance figures beyond approved messaging are omitted.

## What I learned

- Retrieval quality is inseparable from the downstream decision task.
- Shared embedding infrastructure compounds across products.
- Monitoring stale indexes is as important as training better encoders.
